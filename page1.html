Describing the purpose of IT in modern business.

New developments are constantly emerging within the IT industry and these developments affects the business’
ability to communicate with customers speedily and at most hours of the day. Effective communication,
reduced business costs. The speed with which IT is developing means that business’ must try to stay one step ahead
of the competition, or they will become yesterday's news. A smart, attractive website is now the customer's first
point of contact with a business. Couple that with friendly, fast, efficient staff who know what they are doing and can be
relied upon by the customer makes all the difference between failure and success. Today we can communicate right around
the globe in a matter of seconds, a huge difference from the world 30 years ago, the competition to succeed is enormous.



"When customers use technology to interact with a business, the business benefits because better communication
creates a stronger public image. A business that has the technological capacity to research new opportunities will
stay a step ahead of its competition. For a business to survive, it must grow and acquire new opportunities.” Oklahoma SBDC

“Technology has changed the way companies and organizations work to chase their business objectives and goals. They can
accomplish assigned tasks and duties rapidly by using different devices, machines and equipment to increase
productivity & efficiency at workplace.” Technofaq

“Digital projects use the internet and web technologies to deliver anything from websites to mobile apps, videos
to games, eCommerce to emails, social media campaigns to search engine optimization (SEO), rich media advertising to
digital outdoor advertising; We deliver projects in a connected, online world where brands and organizations interact
directly with customers.” The Digital Project Manager
