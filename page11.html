Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites

Copyright is a common problem online. Many people have a way of just copying whatever they find online without realising they might actually be committing an offence. Big businesses take very swift action and take no prisoners, so people need to be far more aware than many of them are. It is always best to seek permission first, especially if there is any doubt. Copyright is virtually automatic provided your work is yours and yours alone. There is a fair use policy, but that appears to vary and there is not a hard and fast rule.
