How would I improve my own productivity using IT in digital projects. If there was a lot to do then I would recommend teamwork as different parts of the project could be divided up between the members of the group. Slack is very good for getting in touch with group members in a private setting, at the same time everyone else in the group is aware of what exactly is going on. Team members with less experience I would have working alongside someone more experienced so they could watch and learn.
