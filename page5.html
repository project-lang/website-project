Describe how you would go about selecting and using IT systems and software. How do you go about selecting the right technology for the job?

What is wanted, compared with what is needed and what is available. Can you afford it? You might have to have members of staff retrained in order to be able to use the new technology, which members of staff could cope with this, or be better suited to using this new model. Will it help profit the company or will it cause a certain amount of disruption? Will the disruption be temporary or longer-term? Talk to the team and appreciate their feedback, they’ll be the ones using this technology on a regular basis. Is it the kind of technology that can keep up with advancements, what sort of maintenance will it require. Ask yourself what your underlying financial model is. How do you plan to make money? You’ll want to find something that is bulletproof, something that fits your budget and doesn't improve one area of your business while disrupting another. Different people will have varying levels of familiarity with the various solutions you'll be considering. When you’re deciding what technology to go with, it’s vital that you have a good understanding of the level of impact it will have on your team in adopting it. Make sure that you know what skills you already have, and what skills or support, you’ll need to find to implement a solution.
